About

This project was drafted by a group of students in order to communicate the presence and importance of the trees within Camden Council. Our means are twofolds: firstly, we visualized the location of "public" trees (trees that are within council-owned property) through a multi-layered map. Secondly, we interpreted council-provided data on the trees through some summaries and a regression analysis.

All information on this website is open to public access. The data was sourced from the Camden Council tree dataset.
